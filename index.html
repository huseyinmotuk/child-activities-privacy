Privacy Policy

Application: Child Activities
Package: com.childactivities.app
Effective date: April 21, 2026
Last updated: April 21, 2026

Child Activities (the "App") is an educational Android application designed for young children (ages 5 and under). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information. The App is built under Google Play's Designed for Families program and is intended to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) of the United States and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the provisions relating to children (GDPR-K).

Summary: The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from its users. There is no account system, no analytics, no crash reporting, and no tracking of any kind. Advertisements are served in a child-directed, non-personalized mode, and an optional one-time in-app purchase is handled entirely by Google Play.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Precise or approximate location data
  • Photos, microphone recordings, or camera input
  • Contacts, calendar entries, or other on-device content
  • Device advertising identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Behavioral analytics, usage telemetry, or crash reports
  • Any data that could be used to identify a child or parent

The App does not contain Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, Google Analytics, or any comparable third-party analytics SDK. No account registration is required or offered.

2. Information Stored Locally on the Device

The App stores a small amount of non-personal data on the device itself, using Android's private SharedPreferences storage, solely to make the App work between sessions:

  • Interface language and learning language selected during onboarding
  • Sound volume, sound on/off, and vibration preference
  • Whether the onboarding flow has been completed
  • Quiz progress (last question index per category)
  • Whether the optional Quiz Mode + Remove Ads purchase has been made (a local cache of the entitlement returned by Google Play)
  • Coloring canvas thumbnails, stored as local PNG files in the App's private storage directory so the child can see their previous work

This data never leaves the device and is not accessible to us. It is removed when the App is uninstalled.

3. Advertising

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob on some screens (Home, Category, Settings, and Coloring). A single interstitial advertisement may be shown once per session when the user exits a category screen.

All ad units in the App are registered with AdMob's child-directed treatment setting enabled. This means:

  • Ads are served in a non-personalized mode.
  • Google does not use the Android advertising identifier to build behavioral profiles of users of this App.
  • Ad content is limited to categories that Google has certified as suitable for children under Google Play's Families policy and the self-certified ad SDK program.

Parents who prefer an ad-free experience can remove all advertisements by purchasing the optional in-app upgrade described in the next section.

For details on how Google AdMob handles data in child-directed apps, see Google's documentation at https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6223431 .

4. In-App Purchase

The App offers a single optional, one-time in-app purchase called "Quiz Mode + Remove Ads" (product ID: quiz_unlock). Purchasing this item unlocks the quiz feature across all categories and removes all advertisements from the App.

The purchase is processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We do not see, receive, or store any payment information — card numbers, billing addresses, Google account details, or purchase tokens. The App stores only a local flag indicating whether the entitlement is active, and re-verifies this flag with Google Play each time the App starts.

The purchase option is placed behind a parental gate in the Settings screen. It is not reachable from any screen a child would normally use, in line with Google Play's Families policy.

5. Permissions

The App requests only the permissions it needs to function:

  • Internet access — used solely to load advertisements from Google AdMob. No other network calls are made.
  • Vibration — used for short haptic feedback when a quiz answer is incorrect. Can be disabled in Settings.
  • Billing — used only when a parent opens the in-app purchase screen, to communicate with Google Play Billing.

The App does not request access to location, camera, microphone, storage, contacts, or any other sensitive permission.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is specifically designed for children. Because the App does not collect any personal information from any user — child or adult — there is no personal information about a child that we could share, sell, or retain. Accordingly:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
  • We do not create profiles of child users.
  • We do not serve personalized advertising to child users.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Parents who have questions about their child's use of the App may contact us at the email address listed below.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Because no personal data is collected on our servers (we do not operate servers that receive user data), there is nothing to retain and nothing to delete on our side. Local data stored on the device (settings, quiz progress, coloring thumbnails) can be erased at any time by:

  • Clearing the App's storage from Android Settings → Apps → Child Activities → Storage → Clear storage, or
  • Uninstalling the App.

8. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated into the App is Google AdMob, used under its child-directed treatment configuration. Google Play Billing is used to process the optional in-app purchase described in Section 4.

Relevant third-party policies:

  • AdMob Families Ads Program: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6223431
